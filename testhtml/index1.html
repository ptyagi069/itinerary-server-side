The Anvaya Beach Resort or Similar
After breakfast, check out and head to Ubud’s famous Sacred Monkey Forest. The Sacred Monkey Forest in Ubud is a must-visit, combining Bali’s natural beauty, cultural heritage, and wildlife encounters. Home to hundreds of playful Balinese macaques, this lush sanctuary also houses ancient temples nestled among towering trees, creating an immersive, serene environment that captures Bali's spiritual essence. After exploring the forest in the afternoon, indulge in a 2-hour spa with a flower bath that offers ultimate relaxation. Bali’s renowned wellness culture shines through in this aromatic flower bath experience, combining essential oils and petals in a warm soak. It’s an ideal way to unwind and recharge, embodying Bali’s famed approach to holistic wellness. Walk: Moderate; includes paths around the Monkey Forest, ensure you carry a comfortable pair of shoes. Facilities: Spa facilities with private rooms, changing rooms, restroom access Highlight: Sacred Monkey Forest, relaxing spa experience with flower bath Overnight: Kuta (Bali) Meal: Breakfast Transfers: Convenient shared transportation in cozy AC coaches or car, letting you soak up Bali's beautiful scenery worry-free. Flower Bath: (Arranged Where Spa Treatment is Offered in Bali) The Flower Bath experience in Bali begins with a 5-minute foot massage, followed by a 1.5-hour body massage with aromatic oils. Afterward, enjoy a 15–20-minute flower bath, where the tub is filled with vibrant petals arranged in patterns, leaving you refreshed and relaxed. The flower petals are arranged in a single layer. For a more personalized experience, a tub is filled with petals, spelling out your name in the water. The experience concludes with a refreshing drink.
